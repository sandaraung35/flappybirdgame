🐦 Flappy Bird Guide

Everything you need to master the game

🎮 How to Play
  • Control the bird character.
  • Click / Tap to fly upward.
  • Release to fall due to gravity.
🎯 Goal
  • Pass through pipe gaps.
  • Earn points for each pipe.
⚠️ Game Over
  • Hit pipe → Game Over
  • Touch ground → Game Over
💡 Tips
  • Keep steady rhythm.
  • Avoid fast clicking.
  • Focus on timing.
